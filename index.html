About Aishwarya

She is a passionate traditional artist specializing in Rangoli design, Mehendi art, Drawing & Painting and Decoration for special moments.


Her artistic journey started through festivals, weddings and family celebrations where she discovered her love for Indian traditional art.


Her mission is to preserve traditional Indian art forms while blending them with modern elegance and creativity.
