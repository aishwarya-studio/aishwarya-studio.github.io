About Aishwarya

She is a passionate traditional artist specializing in Rangoli design, Mehendi art, drawing & Painting and handmade crafts.


My artistic journey started through festivals, weddings and family celebrations where I discovered my love for Indian traditional art.


My mission is to preserve traditional Indian art forms while blending them with modern elegance and creativity.
